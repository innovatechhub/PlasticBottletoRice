Our Purpose

A Mission to Restore & Nourish

At EcoRice, we believe that the solution to plastic pollution shouldn't be a burden, but a bridge to community resilience. Our mission is to transform discarded plastic into a currency for nutrition, creating a self-sustaining cycle that benefits both the planet and its people.

Vision

A world where plastic waste is a relic of the past and food security is a universal reality for every community.

Commitment

We pledge transparency in our impact reporting and 100% ethical sourcing for all our rice distribution programs.

"Every kilogram of plastic collected provides 20 healthy meals."
