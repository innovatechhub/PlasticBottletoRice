Roots of Resilience

Founded in 2022, EcoRice began with a simple question: How do we solve the twin crises of plastic pollution and food insecurity simultaneously?

Our mission is to create a closed-loop system where community members are incentivized to protect their environment. By partnering with local farmers and global recycling tech firms, we turn trash into life-sustaining resources.
